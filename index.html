Economy Class
This is the cheapest fare class on scheduled airlines. Although paying less for the plane ticket, passengers can expect to find the same in-flight comfort as passengers in other classes: adjustable seats, legroom, table on board, etc. The meal on board is adjusted according to the duration and period of the flight: frugal breakfast, lunch, snack, dinner. Passengers are served drinks (coffee, tea, etc.) and can request various devices designed to make their flight more pleasant: head pillows, headphones, tissues, etc. During long flights, airline companies often provide entertainment on board: movies, music, various video programs.
Business class
Business class is the best solution for passengers who want more comfort during flights and who are willing to pay more for additional services. It is offered by most scheduled airlines and, among other things, means:
  • comfortable cabins
  • ergonomic seats with more legroom
First class
This is the highest fare class, passengers traveling in First Class benefit from exclusive services and increased comfort during flights. The chairs are extremely comfortable, equipped with various functions, can be turned into beds, and the tables are available on request and consist of dishes prepared by famous chefs. In comparison, economy class meals are frugal and standardized. At the airport, First Class passengers have access to modern lounges where they can relax away from the hustle and bustle of the terminal.

About Check-in

Airport check-in is the process whereby an airline approves airplane passengers to board an airplane for a flight.
Airlines typically use service counters found at airports for this process, and the check-in is normally handled by an airline itself or a handling agent working on behalf of an airline.

About Check-out

Once you have your boarding pass, look for the signs for departure gates. This will take you through Security and, if necessary, outbound Passport Control.
